Tổng Hợp Cấu Trúc Đồng Nghĩa

1. Câu Điều Kiện & Unless

If ... not ⟷ Unless ... (Trừ khi)

Ví dụ: If he doesn't catch the first metro, he will be late. → Unless he catches the first metro, he will be late.

2. Hiện Tại Hoàn Thành & Quá Khứ Đơn

The last time + S + V2/ed + was + [Thời gian] ⟷ S + hasn't/haven't + V3/ed + since...

Ví dụ: The last time she wrote to her grandparents was last April. → She hasn't written to her grandparents since last April.

3. So sánh với "So" và "Such"

S + be + so + Adj + that... ⟷ It + be + such + (a/an) + Adj + N + that...

Ví dụ: The weather was so awful that we couldn't go picnicking. → It was such awful weather that we couldn't go picnicking.

4. Cấu trúc Suggestion (Gợi ý)

Why don't we...? ⟷ S + suggested + V-ing hoặc suggested that + S + should + V(bare).

Ví dụ: Why don't we collect those empty bottles? → Robert suggested that those empty bottles should be collected.

5. Cấu trúc Cảm xúc / Sở thích

Like / Enjoy ⟷ Be keen on / Be fond of / Be interested in.

Ví dụ: The children like making models of animals in their free time. → The children are keen on making models of animals in their free time.

6. Cấu trúc "It takes"

S + spend + [time] + V-ing ⟷ It takes + [someone] + [time] + to V-inf.

Ví dụ: The students spent ten minutes waiting for their bus. → It took the students ten minutes to wait for their bus.

7. Cấu trúc Because và Because of

Dùng để diễn tả nguyên nhân, lý do. Có 2 cách chuyển đổi phổ biến:

  • Mệnh đề: Because + S + V + O
  • Cụm danh từ: Because of + Noun Phrase / V-ing

Ví dụ:

1. Because he performed well, he was nominated as Singer of the Year.
2. Because of his good performances, he was nominated as Singer of the Year.

8. Cách dùng giới từ sau Nominate

Cấu trúc Cách sử dụng Ví dụ
Nominated as + [Danh hiệu/Chức vụ] Nhấn mạnh vào vị trí, vai trò hoặc danh hiệu được đề cử đảm nhận. He was nominated as Singer of the Year.
Nominated for + [Giải thưởng/Mục đích] Nhấn mạnh vào giải thưởng cụ thể hoặc lý do của cuộc đề cử. He was nominated for the Nobel Prize.

9. Cấu trúc bắt buộc (Must ⟷ Compulsory)

Dùng để viết lại câu diễn tả sự bắt buộc hoặc nội quy. Cấu trúc chuyển đổi:

  • Dùng Must: S + must + V(bare) + O
  • Dùng Compulsory: It is compulsory for + O + to + V(bare)

Ví dụ:

Drivers and passengers must fasten the seat belt.
→ It is compulsory for drivers and passengers to fasten the seat belt.
